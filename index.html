Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the School of Nursing at the University of British Columbia (UBC). My doctoral research explored the role of central sensitization and somatic activating KRAS mutations in endometriosis. Currently, my postdoctoral work is developing a virtual case-based resource about chronic pelvic pain in gynecologic conditions for health care providers in training. My broad research interests include endometriosis, chronic pelvic pain, central sensitization, knowledge translation, and medical education.

For more information about my research, check out my publications.
